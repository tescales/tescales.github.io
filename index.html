Latest Projects

Never Land - Pilot

Dark teen drama set in the 1990s. Based on Peter Pan.

A teenage girl and her autistic brother run away with a group of young musicians hoping to get famous so they can "live forever."

The Question - Pilot

Gender-flip update of a classic character. A self-described "citizen journalist" discovers corruption at the highest levels of Hub City and decides to seek justice on her own. But she has no superpowers - only a blank mask and an unquenchable thirst for Truth. (original character art to the left)

Magnificent - Pilot

A bisexual teenager who lacks confidence suddenly becomes the most powerful man in the Ottoman empire. Based on the life of Suleyman the Magnificent.
